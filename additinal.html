Bitcoin trading involves buying and selling Bitcoin in the market to make a profit from the price fluctuations. Unlike traditional investments where you hold an asset over time, trading is more active and short-term.



Ethereum is a decentralized platform that enables smart contracts and decentralized applications (dApps) to be built and run without any downtime, fraud, control, or interference. Ether (ETH) is the native cryptocurrency used to power the network.



Tether is a stablecoin, meaning it is a type of cryptocurrency designed to maintain a stable value by being pegged to a reserve of assets, usually a fiat currency like the US Dollar.
